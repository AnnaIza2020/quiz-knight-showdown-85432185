
Sound Effects for Quiz Knight Showdown

This directory contains all the sound effects used in the application.

Required sounds:
- success.mp3: Played when a player answers correctly
- fail.mp3: Played when a player answers incorrectly  
- bonus.mp3: Played when a player receives a bonus or special card
- round-start.mp3: Played at the beginning of a new round
- eliminate.mp3: Played when a player is eliminated
- victory.mp3: Played when a player wins the game
- timeout.mp3: Played when the timer runs out
- wheel-spin.mp3: Played when spinning the fortune wheel

You can replace these files with your own sound effects, but keep the filenames the same.
